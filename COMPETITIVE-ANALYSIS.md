```

3. Dentist manually writes prescription (5 min) ← error-prone

K2 DENT AI WORKFLOW:
1. Patient consultation with voice recording (20 min)
2. AI generates anamnesis (30 sec) ← automatic
3. Dentist reviews/edits AI anamnesis (2 min) ← fast
4. AI suggests prescriptions with allergy checks (30 sec) ← safe
5. Dentist validates prescriptions (1 min) ← quick
6. Total: 24 minutes

TIME SAVED: 13 minutes per patient
→ +2-3 extra patients per day
→ +€5000-8000 revenue per month
```
